About Prayer Cards

Welcome to Prayer Cards, a collection of traditional Catholic prayers presented in an interactive card format.

About This Project

This website offers a digital collection of prayer cards featuring traditional Catholic prayers, including everyday prayers, Marian devotions, and prayers to the saints. Each card features beautiful religious artwork and can be flipped to reveal the full prayer text.

How to Use

Click on any prayer card to view the full-sized version. Click the card or use the "Flip Card" button to reveal the prayer on the back. Each card includes an information button (i) to learn more about the artwork featured on the front.

Prayer Categories

Everyday Prayers: Foundational prayers for daily spiritual practice, including the Our Father, Nicene Creed, Morning Offering, and Act of Contrition.

Marian Prayers: Traditional prayers honoring the Blessed Virgin Mary, including ancient antiphons sung during Liturgy of the Hours.

Saints: Prayers to beloved saints, including Saint Francis of Assisi, Saint Michael the Archangel, and Saint Peregrine.

Artwork Attribution

All artwork used in this collection is either in the public domain or used with appropriate licensing. Attribution information is available on each individual card via the information button.
